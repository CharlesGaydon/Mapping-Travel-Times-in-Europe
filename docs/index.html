Hello Dear User !

We are very glad that you found our website ! Here you shall discover a new kind of map.

We have decided to change your world's vision, by helping you to visualize cities according to the trip durations that separate them by train. If you click on your favourite city, the other will be displayed according to their time-closeness.

Feel free to mouse over other cities, it will display the train trip duration between it and your favourite city.

As we are (cool) French students, we focused on the European and French railway networks.
